First Day Issues

These are ordered in the original sequence. The parenthesized numbers after the headings indicate priority, as decided at the time of the discussion.

  1. Empty set: do we need it? (1)
  2. Filled ellipse or not filled? (2)
  3. Semantics of state variables: several unspecified semantics (3)
  4. Shape of a complex & what's under the shape (multiple questions) (4)
    • emek: do shapes have to touch inside? does topology indicate anything?
  5. Shape of compartments (4)
    • can compartments can have any shape?
    • emek: are we allowing nested compartments?
    • emek: are membranes separate compartments?
    • emek: there are neighborhood relationships -- are we going to draw them?
  6. Rectangular shape of gene (2)
    • emek: what happens if several elements of one gene intersect with another? does the info get duplicated?
    • stuart: would like to discuss examples of state transition vis-a-vis gene regulation
  7. Controlled vocabulary for macromolecule labels (1)
    • nicolas: also for physical properties (1)
  8. Modulation, catalysis: use a circle? should catalysis even exist? (5)
    • mirit: do we want all of them in SBGN L1?
    • emek: also trigger and others
    • also topic of shape of modulation & rescaling diagrams
    • anatoly: should there be multiple shapes? or one shape for all? put smth inside the diamond?
  9. All boolean connectors: (5)
    • emek: highly tied to modulation
  10. Module: shape, content, terminals (3)

  11. Hiroaki: discussing reduced notation (1)
  12. Rules for drawing layout (eg avoid overlap, etc.) (1)

  13. Do we want to incorporate annotation glyphs? (1)
  14. Do we want to discuss the languages used to implement SBGN? (0.5)
  15. Do we want to include generic entities in L1? (1)
    • ralph: eg is event
  16. Entity-relationships: how to proceed? (1)

  17. Hiroaki: arrows? (1)
