SBGN-3

The third SBGN meeting will be held on September 30, 2007, in Long Beach, California, at the Hyatt hotel in Long Beach in room “Seaview C”. The meeting is being funded by Japan’s New Energy and Industrial Technology Development Organization. The workshop co-organizers are:

  • Hiroaki Kitano (SBI & Sony CSL)
  • Mike Hucka (Caltech)
  • Nicolas Le Novère (EBI)
  • Akira Funahashi (SBI & JST)

Agenda

The SBGN community has so far developed a still-incomplete draft specification for SBGN Level 1. In this workshop, our aim will be to discuss and settle the remaining issues. In particular, we need to discuss and polish the notation for state-transition diagrams, and discuss entity relationship diagrams.

Venue

The meeting will be held at the Long Beach Hyatt hotel in Long Beach, California.

The meeting room is called “Seaview C” on the 1st floor. Click here for a map of the Hyatt meeting rooms.

You can use the ICSB group code discount for booking a room at the hotel if you wish to stay overnight there. (Of course, if you were planning on staying in another nearby hotel for ICSB, then you may as well stay an extra night at that same hotel.)
